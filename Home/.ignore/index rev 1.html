My Life

I’m a Scottish Youtuber + Twitch Streamer, I'm a bit of a numpty and got a lot of love for retro gaming. Not long started dabbling into game development, currently trying to make a Lego game lol. I’m quite into photo editing and can spend hours on projects either be it making thumbnail art for Youtube, tweaking photographs or making logo art. I have a wee man cave in my house that consists of DJ Decks, a midi keyboard, Lego, and a Studio set up for either music production or video/photo editing. Also have a green screen for chromakey effects. Still dabble in some music creation, when I was 15 I used to produce music a lot and made an album consisting of Trance/Electronica music. I then went into DJ’ing and I used to be a residential DJ in a nightclub before throwing in the towel, during my DJ years I liked making remix’s/bootlegs of songs which I might host on this website. Now I mainly will make music to enhance my game development and video editing, may make some intro music soon for my videos. Streaming or making Youtube videos usually consist of me Video Gaming, I really enjoy playing troll levels from Mario Maker 2 from the Mario Maker 2 community. These videos are usually pretty funny my reactions can be somewhat comical. I also used to make content of me and the misses playing Scary Games, those videos brings priceless reactions lol. Well worth checking out.
